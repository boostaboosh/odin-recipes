Lasagna

Description

This is a traditional lasagna recipe from Italy.

The steps will describe putting together the lasagna, not how to make the ragú or the pasta dough.

Ingredients

  • fresh pasta dough
    • eggs 4/5
    • flour ~400g
  • ragú
    • sausage
    • tomato passata
    • carrot
    • onion
    • olive oil
    • speck optional
  • bechamel sauce
    • butter
    • flour
    • milk

Steps

  1. Get a baking dish to hold your lasagna.
  2. Place a layer of ragú in your baking dish.
  3. Place a layer of bechamel in your baking dish.
  4. Place a layer of semi-cooked pasta dough pieces in your baking dish.
  5. Repeat steps 2 through 4 until your baking dish is full.
  6. Place a final layer of ragú and bechamel on the top of your lasagna
  7. Cook your lasagna in the oven for ~60/70 minutes at ~190/200 °C.
