Fresh Popeye Spinach

Description

This is a simple recipe for making cooked spinach.

Spinach is a super-food and this recipe is so easy you're sure to consume it almost as frequently as Popeye!

Ingredients

  • spinach
  • salt
  • a source of fat optional
    • butter
    • olive oil
    • other source of fat

Steps

  1. Heat up a big saucepan with a lid.
  2. Fit as much spinach as you can in the saucepan while still being able to close the lid of the saucepan.
  3. Add a good amount of salt to the spinach.
  4. Close the lid of the saucepan and let the spinach cook for ~1/2 minutes.
  5. Mix the spinach with a wooden spoon.
  6. Once all the spinach is cooked add your source of fat and place the cooked spinach into a serving dish. Remember to mix up your spinach again if you added a source of fat to it.
